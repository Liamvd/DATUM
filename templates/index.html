Data Annotation, Transformation, and Unified Mapping

Welcome to DATUM, the tool to transform your data into the format that you prefer. Upload your data, choose the format that you want and generate the results. When you get your desired results, export the data or send it to a data capture tool of your choosing.

Data upload

Choose with data to upload. Data can be uploaded through: 1. Choosing a local file, 2. Submitting a link, 3. Pasting plain text. Multiple datasets can be uploaded at once, so add as many as you prefer. Once all the data has been inputted, click the 'Upload' button.
